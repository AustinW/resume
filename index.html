About Me

I am currently a senior student at California State University, Fullerton studying Computer Science. There, I have branched off from my experience in PHP to Python, Javascript, Objective-C, and many other web programming and database technologies. I currently work primarily in PHP, developing a large scale API capable of serving millions of HTTP requests. I have worked for a startup company, Homefree, creating a revolutionary real estate product, empowering users to perform real estate transactions without the constraint of real estate agents and without any commission collected from the site/app. We used Laravel extensively to provide an interface for our iPhone app to connect and perform a multitude of operations including manipulating information in a MySQL database, integrating with social services, sending Apple push notifications, queuing email notifications using Redis (php-resque), performing geographical searches on properties, and many other features.
